Public Transit

If you google Hanoi traffic online, there is a high chance that you will feel intimidated by the flow of cars mixed with bustling motorbikes. Once you land, that quite frightening picture will be worsened with the level of noise and smoke.

However, don’t be misled! Transportation to, in and around Hanoi will be an experience you cannot find in any other capital in the world.

In fact, it is fairly easy to arrive at, as well as to move around Hanoi. The public transportation system is excellent, such as trains and buses. Otherwise taxi and motor-taxi services are readily available in any corner of the street.

As you read more about Hanoi, you will be pleased that transportation is one of the city’s major advantage. Read about Hanoi Bus for an idea about taking public transit in Hanoi.
